Mean curvature flow and geometric measure theory

A new canonical space-time-Grassmann measure recasts Brakke flow as a space-time measure satisfying a distributional Brakke inequality. This refines measurability and tangential-structure tools (mean curvature vector, density, tangent maps), sharpening GMT foundations for analyzing singularities and blow-up in mean curvature flow. * The space-time-Grassmann measure of the Brakke flow by Yu Tong Liu, Myles Workman
