Stability and Index of Minimal Hypersurfaces in Special Einstein Manifolds

  • Stability of Homogeneous minimal hypersurfaces in the Page space and (Y^{p,q}) Sasaki-Einstein manifolds by Natalia Gherghel, Hari K. Kunduri

These are explicit stability and spectrum computations for homogeneous minimal hypersurfaces in Einstein backgrounds (Page space ( \mathbb{CP}^2 # \overline{\mathbb{CP}^2} ) and ( Y^{p,q} ) Sasaki–Einstein). Knowing the full index and Jacobi spectrum for concrete families gives precise test cases for min–max theory, informs expected multiplicities/instability in variational constructions, and can guide gluing or desingularization schemes where control of the stability operator is crucial.

Fractional Phase Transitions and Nonlocal Mean Curvature Flow

  • The strongly nonlocal Allen-Cahn problem by Erisa Hasani, Stefania Patrizi

First rigorous sharp-interface convergence for the fractional Allen–Cahn dynamics in the strongly nonlocal regime ( s \in (0,\tfrac{1}{2}) ): ( \varepsilon \partial_t u^\varepsilon = \mathcal{I}^s_n[u^\varepsilon] - \varepsilon^{-2s} W'(u^\varepsilon) ), with interfaces converging to fractional mean curvature flow. This closes the gap with the ( s \ge \tfrac{1}{2} ) case and strengthens the bridge between phase-transition approximations and nonlocal minimal hypersurfaces, expanding the toolbox for studying singularity formation, comparison principles, and barrier constructions in nonlocal flows.

Fractional GMT: Tangents and Blow-ups

  • On blow-ups of sets with finite fractional variation by Giorgio Stefani

A fractional-De Giorgi type tangent classification: for sets ( E \subset \mathbb{R}^N ) of locally finite fractional ( \alpha )-variation, blow-ups with locally finite integer perimeter are half-spaces oriented by the fractional inner normal at ( |D^\alpha \mathbf{1}_E| )-a.e. point. This sharpens the structural theory for nonlocal perimeters and supports regularity programs for nonlocal minimal surfaces, with clear implications for monotonicity, rectifiability, and tangent-plane uniqueness in fractional GMT.

Fractional Curvature Equations and Conformal Geometry

  • Classification of fractional, singular Yamabe metrics on a twice punctured sphere I by João Henrique Andrade, Azahara DelaTorre, João Marcos do Ò, Jesse Ratzkin, Juncheng Wei

Classifies Delaunay-type solutions for constant fractional ( Q )-curvature on a twice-punctured sphere for ( s \lesssim 1 ), including sharp a priori bounds. Techniques (blow-up analysis, ODE reduction, variational characterizations) are adjacent to those used in singularity and compactness studies for geometric PDEs, though the setting is conformal rather than minimal surface theory.

Variational Models and GMT Tools in Transport

  • Variational models of robust optimal transport by Luigi De Masi, Andrea Marchese, Annalisa Massaccesi

Introduces Eulerian and Lagrangian formulations for robust OT using rectifiable measures, normal currents, and traffic plans, proving existence of minimizers. While the application is transport networks, the framework leverages GMT (rectifiability, currents) and may offer transferable ideas for network-like minimal structures or numerical formulations that mirror varifold/current approaches.
